Biography

Evangelist Denielle Barnhardt is an anointed preacher, teacher, and psalmist whose gifts extend into many streams in the body of Christ. She has been recognized in the arenas of theatre, music, and ministry for her hard work and dedication to the furtherance of gospel message of freedom and joy. She teaches Musical Theater, and Special education Science and Social Studies in the East Orange School Districtt. She is a second generation preacher who operates in power and demonstration. She was licensed under the leadership of Pastor Carrie Lee Huggins in 2006 at the tender age of 16, and since then her gifts and anointing has grown and has touched the lives of many. In the year of 2011, Evangelist Denielle married the Love of her life, Minister Jaquan Barnhardt, and to this union was born two sons, Jalon Barnhardt and Jonah Barnhardt. The Barnhardts currently serve beside each other at The Bethel Church of Love and Praise CKMI where they are fully submitted to the leadership of Bishop Charles W. Harris Jr. Evangelist Denielle has had an extensive academic career and is currently looking forward to her graduation in May 2016 from Liberty University in Lynchburg, VA with her degree in Biblical Education and Church Leadership. Evangelist Denielle is a young woman who is led by the Spirit of God and is working on many things. Denielle was blessed to have released her mixtape entitled “Music back to Sender” in 2011, and is currently anticipating the completion of her books entitled “ReWorded: A Devotional for Tweens and Teens,” and a children’s fable “A Dog on a Leash.” We are confident that God is not finished the work He has to do in and for Evangelist Denielle Barnhardt. She has built her ministry on the words found in Leviticus 19:18 “'Do not seek revenge or bear a grudge against anyone among your people, but love your neighbor as yourself. I am the LORD.”
